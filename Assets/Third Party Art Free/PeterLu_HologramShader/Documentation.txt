
Basic Rim Shader Use Guidelines:

Properties:
    _Color          			:The base color of the material, which determines the Albedo of the material
	_RimPower       			:The number that control the power of the rim effect, the bigger the number, the more intense rim effect
	_RimColor       			:The rim color of the material, which determines the color of the rim effect

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Basic Hologram shader Use Guidelines:

Properties:
    	_Color                  :The base color of the material, which determines the Albedo of the material
		_HoloColor              :The color of the hologram
		_HoloValue              :The value of the hologram, the higher the number, the more lines will show up on the model
		_HoloDistance           :The gap between hologram lines, the bigger the number, the smaller the gap
		_HoloDirection          :The direction of the hologram lines
		_EmissionMultiplier     :The emission value multiplier

		_GlitchStrength         :The strength of the glitch effect, the bigger the number, the more intense of the effect
		_GlitchDensity          :The density of the glitch effect
		_GlitchPeriod           :The time period of the glitch effect, the bigger the number, the faster the effect shows
		_GlitchDirection        :The direction of the glitch effect
